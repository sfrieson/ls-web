Current Work

Leslie Satin writes: I am working on a dance whose structure is derived from a series of compositional scores which I have used in several previous dances; the movement material itself and the organization of sections are entirely new. Its working title, Resume/Résumé, points to the personal, chronological, and choreographic phenomena embedded in my exploration of space, place, and experience.

Most recently (June 2024), I showed a new work at Arts on Site (NYC). The piece, Fine Lines, was a duet for Jeremy Nelson and me, accompanied by Connie Beckley’s reading of a text (mine) describing six black and white photographs, none seen by the audience.
