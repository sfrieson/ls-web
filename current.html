Current Work

RESUME/RÉSUMÉ

Leslie Satin writes: I am showing a new dance, Resume/Résumé, whose structure is derived from a series of compositional scores which I have used in several previous dances; the movement material itself and the organization of sections are entirely new. Its title, points to the personal, chronological, and choreographic phenomena embedded in my exploration of space, place, and experience.

Resume/Résumé will show at Arts on Site (12 Saint Marks Place, New York, NY), October 4th and 5th at 7:30 p.m. Find tickets on the Arts on Site website.

Most recently (June 2024), I showed a new work at Arts on Site (NYC). The piece, Fine Lines, was a duet for Jeremy Nelson and me, accompanied by Connie Beckley’s reading of a text (mine) describing six black and white photographs, none seen by the audience.
